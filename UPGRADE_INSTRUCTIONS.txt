NOURIVOLT FUTURE VISION UPGRADE

KEEP YOUR EXISTING DATA

Your accounts and logs are stored in:
    data\nourivolt.db

Before upgrading:
1. Stop Streamlit.
2. Copy data\nourivolt.db to a safe backup location.
3. Replace app.py, requirements.txt, README.md, and .streamlit\config.toml.
4. Add vision_services.py.
5. Put your original nourivolt.db back inside the data folder.
6. Run: pip install -r requirements.txt
7. Run: streamlit run app.py

The app creates the new Smart Scan and Readiness tables automatically.
Do not replace your existing database with an empty database.
