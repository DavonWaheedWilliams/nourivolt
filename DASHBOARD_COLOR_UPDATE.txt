NouriVolt dashboard color-progress update

The app's existing functionality remains unchanged.

Dashboard visual changes:
- Calories ring fills purple as calories are logged.
- Protein ring fills teal as protein is logged.
- Water ring fills blue as water is logged.
- Readiness ring fills according to the score and changes color by readiness range.
- Carbohydrates bar fills orange to yellow as carbohydrates are logged.
- Fat bar fills pink to violet as fat is logged.
- Progress fills animate whenever the dashboard refreshes.

Keep your existing data/nourivolt.db file when replacing app files.
