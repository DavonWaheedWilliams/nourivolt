Dashboard ring fill correction

The four circular dashboard metrics now use direct conic-gradient progress fills.
The colored arc equals the completed percentage and the remaining arc stays light gray.
Zero values display a fully gray ring.

Examples:
- 100 of 1,800 calories = 5.6% colored
- 25 of 225 grams protein = 11.1% colored
- 0 water = gray ring
- Readiness follows the 0-100 score

All existing app functionality and saved-data compatibility remain unchanged.
